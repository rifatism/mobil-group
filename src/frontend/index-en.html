GPS/GLONASS Fleet Monitoring

Installation of certified satellite navigation equipment in compliance with Russian Government Decree No. 2216. Mandatory for passenger buses (M2, M3) and heavy goods vehicles (N2, N3) carrying hazardous cargo. Vehicle data is transmitted to the state JSC GLONASS monitoring system in real time.

  • Compliance with RF Decree No. 2216 and Ministry of Transport Order No. 440
  • Data transfer to the state JSC GLONASS system
  • Online tracking of route, speed, and events
  • Fuel control and driver activity monitoring
  • Fines for non-compliance: up to 500,000 RUB for legal entities
Video Recorders & Video Analytics

Professional systems supporting up to 16 cameras with on-board AI analytics. Operating range −50…+70°C. Compliant with RF Decree No. 969 (certification from 01.09.2026).

  • ADAS: lane departure, dangerous proximity, pedestrian detection
  • DSM: fatigue, distraction, and phone use monitoring
  • Archive retention 3+ months (8 cameras, 2 TB HDD)

Tachography

Installation, maintenance, and SKZI (cryptographic security module) replacement on Russian-standard tachographs. Processing of driver and enterprise cards. Required for trucks over 3.5 t (N2, N3) and buses with more than 8 seats (M2, M3). The SKZI module must be replaced every 3 years.

  • Russian tachographs with SKZI (SHTRIKH-TakhoRUS, ATOL Drive 5)
  • SKZI module replacement every 3 years (from 34,500 RUB)
  • Driver cards (5,200 RUB) and enterprise cards (6,200 RUB)
  • Verification, calibration, and registration with Rosavtotrans
  • Fines for missing tachograph: up to 50,000 RUB for legal entities
About the SKZI Module

The cryptographic security module encrypts all driver activity and trip data in a tamper-proof format. After 3 years the module locks and must be replaced.

  • Expired SKZI = fine equal to missing tachograph
  • Activation via the Rosavtotrans registry
  • In-person appointment and original documents required

Satellite Communications Iridium / VSAT

Reliable connectivity in remote locations where GSM coverage is unavailable: oil fields, taiga, Siberia, and the Arctic. Iridium technology provides 100% global Earth coverage; VSAT delivers broadband corporate internet to isolated facilities.

  • Iridium — voice and data worldwide with 100% coverage
  • VSAT — high-speed internet at remote facilities
  • SOS signalling and emergency buttons for crews
  • Applications: oil & gas, forestry, Arctic operations
  • Integration with GPS/GLONASS fleet monitoring systems
When It's Needed

Essential for enterprises operating beyond GSM coverage or requiring guaranteed connectivity independent of ground infrastructure.

  • Oil and gas fields in Siberia and the Arctic
  • Forestry operations deep in the taiga
  • Vehicles on routes beyond cellular network coverage
